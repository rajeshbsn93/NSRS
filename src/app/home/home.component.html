About of NSRS

National Sports Repository System (NSRS) is a digital MIS, which envisages to provide a comprehensive digital solution for all the major stakeholders such as Athletes, Coaches, Sports Scientists, Sports Training Centers, Federations and Administrators. It is an open platform of sports eco-system where Athletes, Coaches and Academies can register themselves. On successful registration, an Unique number (KID - Khelo India ID) is generated, which is used for accessing the NSRS.
